CyberShield-spectrum

Tools: Burp Suit, Snort, Sysmon, Wireshark, Tcpdump and Volatility3

This project simulates a complete cybersecurity environment for learning ethical hacking and defense. It involves building a virtual network, performing penetration testing, configuring firewalls and VPNs, and deploying intrusion detection systems. It also covers threat hunting and digital forensics using tools like Snort, Sysmon, and Volatility3, providing hands-on experience across the full cyber defense lifecycle.
